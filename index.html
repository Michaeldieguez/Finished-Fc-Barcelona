F.C. Barcelona

Spain's F.C. Barcelona

My favorite team is a club in Spain called F.C. Barcelona. Their stadium is called Camp Nou that has 99.354 seats in the stadium. They have 28 Spanish cup Winners, 11 Spanish Super Cup Winner, 24 Spanish Champion,8 Catalunya Winner, 5 DfB Supercup Sieger,4 Cup Winners Cup Winner, 4 Copa Eva Duarte Winner, 3 FIFA Club Champion Clubs' Cup. They had a lot of legends in the past and even now.

The Club was established on November 29th, 1899. Joan Gamper was the founder of the Football Club Barcelona. He chose the famouse club color too which was blue and claret. Some of the soccer legends that signed up for the club are Ladislau Kubala, Johan Cruyff, Diego Maradona, Ronaldhino and Leonel Messi. Leonel Messi is still playing for the club. He has played in this club in his whole career. Barcelona's motto is Mes Que Un Club (More Than A Club).
